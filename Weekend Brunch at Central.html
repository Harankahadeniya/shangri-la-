Let Central, our vibrant all-day dining restaurant with our team of expert chefs present a wide-ranging selection of international dishes allowing your tastebuds travel across India, Japan, Southeast Asia, and the West, whilst also celebrating Sri Lanka’s tapestry of native cuisine.

With 16 live action stations, each food terminal with its dynamic show kitchen represents a stop-over on this culinary destination tour around the globe that is well-worth navigating.

Priced at LKR 8,510 nett per person every Saturday and Sunday from 12.30 pm to 3.30 pm.
