Explore Sri Lanka

Sunset Cruise

Experience the magic of a Sri Lankan sunset: As the sun slowly sets over the horizon, the city skyline is bathed in a golden glow. The surrounding mountains also take on a fiery hue, creating a truly unforgettable sight. You can also enjoy the cool evening breeze and the sound of the waves gently lapping against the shore.

This cruise takes you on a relaxing journey along the Kelani River, where you can enjoy the stunning views of the city skyline and the surrounding mountains. You can also enjoy a delicious buffet dinner and complimentary drinks.

Tuk Tuk safari

Explore the bustling streets of Colombo on a tuk-tuk ride, or take a walk through the Pettah Market to experience the hustle and bustle of this vibrant city. Visit the Gangaramaya Temple, a Buddhist temple with a beautiful garden, or the National Museum, which houses a collection of artifacts from Sri Lanka's history

Day Trip To Galle

Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Galle Fort, a beautifully preserved colonial town. Take a walk along the ramparts, explore the narrow streets, and visit the lighthouse. In the afternoon, relax on the beach in Unawatuna.
