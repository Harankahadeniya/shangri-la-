Dining

Dine at our finest restaurants

The Grill Room is a classic steakhouse located in the Shangri-La Hotel. It offers a wide selection of steaks, chops, and seafood, all grilled to perfection. The menu also includes a variety of side dishes, such as creamed spinach, mashed potatoes, and asparagus.

The Grill Room is a popular choice for special occasions, such as birthdays, anniversaries, and business dinners. The restaurant has a sophisticated atmosphere, with dark wood paneling and leather upholstery. The service is impeccable, and the staff is knowledgeable about the menu.

Horizon Lounge

Located on the 37th floor and reserved exclusively for Horizon Club or suite room guests, our elegant club rounge is designed as a home-away-from-home for busy globetrotters. Decorated in a contemporary international style, this cozy urban haven also offers some of the best panoramic views over the city. Thoughtfully arranged intimate seating areas are complemented with meticulous full-service support from a specialist concierge team.

Cafe Central

Inspired by the train journeys around Sri Lanka, Central at Shangri-La Colombo welcomes you to step aboard and embark on a gastronomic culinary journey across South Asia as you celebrate Sri Lanka’s and the regions tapestry of vibrant flavours and aromas.Central’s two semi-private dining rooms are perfect for an intimate evening celebrating with friends, families or colleagues.
